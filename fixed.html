Adopt, Don’t Shop is a phrase used by animal advocates encouraging those welcoming new animals into their lives to consider adoption over purchasing an animal from a breeder or pet store.

Adopt, Don’t Shop is not a phrase used to belittle animal owners who have gotten their furry friends from breeders or pet stores. We understand adoption is not possible for everyone due to allergies and other health related issues.

BENEFITS OF ADOPTION

Heart set on a breed? Research special breed rescue organizations

Shelter animals are not “broken”, most are victims of bad situations.

Some shelters spay and neuter animals before adoption, saving you money on vet bills.

Adopting an animal typically costs less than purchasing an animal from a breeder or pet store.

Your money goes back to the shelter so they can continue to care for current and future animals.
